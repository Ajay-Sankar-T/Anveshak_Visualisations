Key insight: The contact point is always at distance Rr from D (on B's surface) and at distance Rh from C (on A's hole wall). Since both are at radius Rr from D, velocity matching gives θ̇ = ω₁+ω₂ — independent of Rh and Rr individually. Toggle off pure rolling to see slip develop.
